Admission Requirements

Medicine Department

  • High School Diploma with minimum GPA of 3.5
  • Strong background in Biology and Chemistry
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Entrance examination scores
  • Personal statement

Agriculture Department

  • High School Diploma with minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Background in Biology and Environmental Science
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement
  • Interview
