Program Overview

Medical Degree

A 5-year comprehensive medical education program designed to equip students with foundational knowledge in anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, and clinical practices. Students participate in hands-on experiences through internships and rotations in leading hospitals, preparing them for careers in general medicine or specialized fields.

Specializations

After completing core medical training, students can pursue specializations in areas like cardiology, neurology, pediatrics, and more. Each specialization offers in-depth study and practical training guided by industry-leading experts to develop advanced expertise.

Research Programs

Engage in groundbreaking medical research addressing real-world health challenges. Students collaborate with faculty on projects in areas such as immunology, genetics, and public health, gaining valuable insights and contributing to scientific advancements.
