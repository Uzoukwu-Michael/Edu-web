Admission Requirements

Your Journey to Excellence Starts Here

Academic Requirements

  • High School Diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Standardized test scores
  • Academic transcripts

Language Requirements

  • TOEFL score: Minimum 80
  • IELTS score: Minimum 6.5
  • English proficiency certificate
  • Language interview (if applicable)

Medicine Specific Requirements

  • Minimum GPA of 3.5 in Science subjects
  • MCAT scores
  • Biology and Chemistry prerequisites
  • Healthcare experience

Additional Requirements

  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement
  • Interview
  • Health clearance

Agriculture Specific Requirements

  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Biology and Environmental Science background
  • Agricultural experience (preferred)
  • Research project (if applicable)

Additional Requirements

  • Letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose
  • Interview
  • Portfolio (if applicable)
